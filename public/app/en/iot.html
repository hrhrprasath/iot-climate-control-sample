Watson IoT Platform

Description

Watson IoT Platform is the core of any IoT solution and lets your apps communicate with and consume data collected by your connected devices, sensors, and gateways. In this solution, Watson IoT Platform is used to define device types, connect devices, and manage the flow of device data.

Sample IoT Solution for Climate Control

Create a thermostat device type in IBM Watson IoT Platform.

  1. Click Go to IoT Dashboard on IBM Bluemix.
  2. If youâ€™re not logged into Bluemix, Log in now.
  3. Click on the Internet of Things Platform service tile labeled iottool-IoT-devx.
  4. On the bottom left of the service details page, click the Launch Dashboard button.
  5. In the Watson IoT Platform dashboard, go to Devices and then select the Device Types tab.
  6. Click Create Type.
  7. Click Create device type.
  8. In the name field, enter Thermostat.
  9. Click Next three times to skip the Define Template section.
  10. Click Create. Note: You do not need to add any metadata.
  11. Verify that your Thermostat device type was successfully added.
  12. Back on the IoT for Climate Control landing page, click Next.
