Solution Description

Your Sample IoT solution for Climate Control is deployed

Use this solution to create an end-to-end solution for climate control by using IBM Watson IoT Platform with Node-RED and its Device Manager Nodes and simulation nodes. The solution is designed to help a retail company manage the climate in a number of stores that have multiple rooms in each store. The rooms must stay in a certain temperature range, because temperatures that are too high result in customers leaving, and temperatures that are too low result in excessive power usage.

To prevent these kinds of scenarios, all rooms are equipped with thermostats that are connected to Watson IoT Platform. If the temperature exceeds the maximum permissible temperature, an alarm event is sent to the service engineer for quick action.

Components

The Sample IoT solution for Climate Control is composed of three major components. Watson IoT Platform, Node-Red, and a device simulator.

Watson IoT Platform

Watson IoT Platform lets your apps communicate with and consume data from your connected devices, sensors, and gateways.

Node-RED

Node-RED is a tool for wiring together hardware devices, APIs, and online services in new and interesting ways.

Device Simulator

Use the device simulator to simulate device instances, connect the devices to Watson IoT Platform, and generate device data.

Climate Control Alert -End User App

Use the end-user app to set up email notifications that are based on room temperature
